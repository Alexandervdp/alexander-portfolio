Award Winner

Biomechanical Ornithopter

As my final year Bachelor's project, my colleague (Tibo Opdebeeck) and I took on the challenge of biomechanically replicating a bird's flight. We developed multiple prototypes that simulated the complex wing flap as accurately as possible, combining mechanics, electronics, and aerodynamics. All components were custom-designed, 3D-printed, and lasercut by us.

Innovation

Simulated biomechanics using integrated mechanics, electronics, and aerodynamics.
